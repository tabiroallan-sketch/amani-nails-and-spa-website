Your wedding day is one of the most photographed moments of your life. Here's our week-by-week guide to ensuring you glow from every angle.

**6 Weeks Before — Start Your Facial Journey**

Begin a series of customised facials every 3–4 weeks. This allows your skin to cycle through purging and emerge clear. We recommend our Brightening Facial for that wedding-day luminosity.

**4 Weeks Before — Finalise Your Nail Shape**

If you're getting acrylics or gel extensions, have them done now. This gives you time to adjust to the length and shape before the big day.

**2 Weeks Before — Deep Tissue Massage**

Stress peaks in the final two weeks. A deep tissue massage releases tension held in the shoulders, neck, and back — where brides carry the most stress.

**1 Week Before — Bridal Radiance Package**

This is the moment. Our Bridal Radiance Package combines:
- A hot stone massage to melt final nerves
- A brightening facial for instant radiance
- Gel manicure and pedicure in your wedding colour

**The Night Before**

Hydrate, hydrate, hydrate. Drink water, skip alcohol, and get a full night's sleep. Apply a thick layer of hand cream and wear cotton gloves overnight.

**The Morning Of**

Arrive at Amani for a light brow tidy, a touch-up facial mist, and a moment of stillness before the celebration begins.
