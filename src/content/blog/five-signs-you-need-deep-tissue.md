Your body has a language all its own. Before pain becomes chronic, it whispers. Here are five signs it's time to book a deep tissue session.

**1. You Wake Up with Stiffness Every Morning**

If your first steps out of bed are accompanied by tightness in your lower back or shoulders, your muscles are carrying tension from the day before — and not releasing it during sleep.

**2. You Hold Stress in Your Shoulders**

Notice your shoulders creeping toward your ears during a workday? Chronic tension in the trapezius and levator scapulae muscles can lead to tension headaches and restricted neck mobility.

**3. Your Range of Motion Is Decreasing**

Can't turn your head as far during reversing? Finding it harder to reach overhead? Reduced flexibility is a clear sign that fascia and muscle fibres have adhered and need manual release.

**4. You Exercise Regularly but Don't Recover Well**

Athletes and fitness enthusiasts — if your muscles feel perpetually heavy or sore, deep tissue work can break up lactic acid buildup and improve blood flow to speed recovery.

**5. You've Been Sitting More Than Usual**

Desk jobs, long commutes, and Netflix marathons all shorten the hip flexors and tighten the chest. Deep tissue massage counteracts the "sitting posture" before it becomes permanent.
